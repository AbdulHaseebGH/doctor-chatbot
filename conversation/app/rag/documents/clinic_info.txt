City Medical Clinic is located at 123 Medical Drive, Springfield. The clinic is open Monday through Saturday from 9am to 6pm. The clinic is closed on Sundays and public holidays. You can reach the clinic by phone at (555) 123-4567.

The clinic accepts walk-in patients during business hours. Appointments are recommended for specialists. Emergency cases should call 911 or go to the nearest emergency room immediately.

Parking is available in the clinic parking lot free of charge for patients. The clinic is accessible by public transport — Bus Route 12 stops directly outside the clinic entrance.

The clinic accepts most major health insurance plans. Patients should bring their insurance card and a valid photo ID to every appointment. Self-pay options are available with payment plans for eligible patients.

To book, change, or cancel an appointment, patients can call (555) 123-4567 or speak to Sara the AI receptionist. Appointment confirmation is sent via SMS or email upon request.
