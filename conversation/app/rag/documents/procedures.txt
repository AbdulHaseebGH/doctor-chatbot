Medical procedures and services offered at City Medical Clinic:

Blood Tests and Lab Work:
The clinic can order routine blood tests including complete blood count (CBC), blood sugar (glucose), cholesterol levels, thyroid function (TSH/T3/T4), liver function tests, kidney function tests, and more. Blood samples are collected at the clinic and sent to an affiliated laboratory. Results are typically available within 48–72 hours.

X-Rays:
Basic X-rays are available on-site. X-rays are commonly ordered for suspected fractures, chest conditions, or joint problems. Dr. Ahmed or Dr. Khan may order X-rays during your consultation.

ECG / EKG (Electrocardiogram):
Dr. Khan performs ECG tests at the clinic to assess heart rhythm and detect cardiac conditions. This is a non-invasive test that takes approximately 10 minutes.

Vaccinations and Immunizations:
Dr. Alina offers childhood vaccination schedules including MMR, polio, hepatitis B, and flu vaccines. Adult flu vaccines and travel vaccinations are also available. Please call ahead to confirm vaccine availability.

Pediatric Services:
Dr. Alina provides newborn check-ups, infant well-baby visits, school physical exams, developmental screenings, and adolescent health consultations.

Specialist Referrals:
If your condition requires care beyond what the clinic can provide, the doctor will write a referral letter to an appropriate specialist. Common referrals include orthopedics, neurology, dermatology, and gastroenterology.

Prescription Services:
Doctors can prescribe medications during or after your consultation. Prescriptions can be filled at any pharmacy. The clinic does not operate a pharmacy on-site.

Annual Physical Exam:
Routine annual check-ups are available with Dr. Ahmed. These include general health assessment, blood pressure measurement, weight monitoring, and review of any ongoing conditions.
