Common symptoms and which doctor to see at City Medical Clinic:

Fever, cold, flu, sore throat, cough, runny nose: See Dr. Ahmed (General Practitioner), available Monday–Saturday.

Headache, migraine: See Dr. Ahmed for initial assessment. If recurring, Dr. Ahmed may refer you to a neurologist.

Chest pain, heart palpitations, shortness of breath, high blood pressure: This may be a cardiac emergency. If severe, call 911 immediately. If mild and non-emergency, see Dr. Khan (Cardiologist) on Tuesday, Thursday, or Saturday.

Stomach pain, nausea, vomiting, diarrhea: See Dr. Ahmed (General Practitioner).

Rash, skin irritation, allergies: See Dr. Ahmed for initial consultation. He may refer to a dermatologist if needed.

Back pain, joint pain, muscle pain: See Dr. Ahmed. He can prescribe pain management and refer to a physiotherapist.

Children's health: fever in children, ear infections, vaccinations, growth concerns: See Dr. Alina (Pediatrician) on Monday, Wednesday, or Friday.

Dizziness, fainting, loss of consciousness: Seek immediate emergency care if severe. For recurring mild dizziness, see Dr. Ahmed.

Fatigue, weight changes, thyroid concerns: See Dr. Ahmed who can order blood tests and refer if needed.

Prescription refills: Contact the clinic at (555) 123-4567 or visit during clinic hours. Bring previous prescription or medication name.
